About Me

i grew up in the great state of connecticut in the great town of avon. in high school, i was an avid participant in math competitions, where i discovered my love for math and all of its relatives. i'm not as involved as i used to be anymore (placed top 200 putnam but that's pretty much it), but i always enjoy the occasional problem.

competition math aside, i'm currently a software engineering intern at snapsheet, where i revamped a rubygem that we use to make yaml storage and querying compatible with rails admin. i'm also a TA for the computer science department: in spring 2023, i was part of the cs1420 (machine learning) course staff, and now i'm part of the cs1570 (design and analysis of algorithms) team.

if it wasn't obvious by this point, i'm pursuing a double bs.c. in computer science and mathematics, focusing on theory and ml for the former. i have a huge love for both, so i'm extremely grateful to brown's open curriculum for letting me explore these subjects to the fullest.
